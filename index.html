Smt L V (Govt) Polytechnic College, Hassan

OUR HISTORY
70 YEARS OF LEGACY

.Est. 1934 - Hassan

Smt. L.V. Government Polytechnic (SLVGP) is one of the oldest and most prestigious government polytechnic institutions in Karnataka. Established in the mid-20th century, the institutions has steadily evolved into a center of technical excellence, producing thousands of skilled engineers and professional who have made significat contributions across various industries in india.

The college is situated on the scenic outskirts of Hassan city, near KMF Dairy on the Bangalore-Mangolre Road, approximately 3 kilometers from the Hassan Bus Stand. The Campus spans 27 acres and offers a calm, spacious, and academically conducive environment.

~1948

INSTITUTION FOUNDED

Established as one of Karnataka's earliest government polytechnics, dedicated to technical education for the region.

~1957

Diploma Courses Launched

Formal 3-year diploma programmes in engineering specialisations began, setting the foundation for technical training.

SMRUTHI MANDARA
ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

Smruthi Mandara - meaning “The flower of Memories” - is the official alumni association of Smt. L.V. (Govt) Polytechnic, Hassan. The association serves as a vital link connecting generations of SLVGP graduates, fostering a spirit of brotherhood, services, and gratitude toward their alma mater.

One of the association’s most significant contributions is the established of the college cafeteria, ensuring that students and staff have access to safe, hygienic, and affordable meals on campus. This initiative stands as a testament to the alumni’s generosity, commitment, and proactive leadership.

Through Smruthi Mandara, alumni actively contribute to campus development, mentor current students, facilitate industry connections, and help preserve and celebrate the institution’s rich technical heritage.
